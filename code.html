Failing Schools Flowchart

My solution to a story dealing with an extremely complex law was this: design a clear, informative flowchart that correctly outlines the new law for our community audience. On mobile, the flowchart displays slightly different text in order to communicate the same points with a separate design (as a complex flowchart would not work well on a mobile screen). The flowchart was well-received and resulted in community requests for a PDF version to use at meetings (which I obliged).

JavaScript. Scss.

Map of Texas Emergency Centers

In Texas, there's a "Baby Moses" law that allows a parent to safely abandon a newborn at some sort of care center, if they cannot provide for the child. A new law included freestanding emergency centers in this list of dropoff centers. I used a clustermap to show where these centers were, after grabbing the addresses from an Excel file and using Google Fusion Tables and Google Earth to find their lat and long.

Excel. Fusion Tables. CSVKit. Google Earth. MapBox.

Breakdown: Texas County Jails Deaths

Almost immediately after the Sandra Bland story broke, a reporter and I requested the data of deaths in Texas jails. I worked on a team to visualize the race and gender make-up of deaths and suicides at Texas county jails, as well as the type of items used. As the manner of Bland's death (a trashbag) had been debated, it was important to show if there were any other instances of trashbag suicides, as well as what other sorts of objects were used in Texas county jails to commit hangings.

Excel. Font Awesome. Scss.

At Majority-Minority Schools, Confederate Names Remain

Debate over the Confederate flag had just erupted, and in the first week of my Texas Tribune internship I pitched a story about the demographics of Texas schools named after Confederate figures. After combing through data and calling schools to verifying their name origins, I used Texas Education Agency data to visualize the racial make-up of children attending these schools. In the end, I verified 29 public schools in Texas named after prominent Confederate figures, 23 of which were majority-minority. Each school graphic included the number of students attending it for context.

Excel. CSVkit. Scss.

Romaine

Romaine is an initiative I and three friends (Ashley Wu, Suyeon Son and Nicole Zhu) worked on during our fall 2014 fellowship with Northwestern University Knight Lab. It's an app that uses NU's course API and Facebook's friends API to allow users to choose upcoming classes for registration based on the ones which their friends have chosen. The project was led by Son and Zhu, while the majority of my contribution to this app was based in its User Experience and Search Design.

JavaScript. CSS.
